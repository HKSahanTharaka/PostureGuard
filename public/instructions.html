Instructions

Prior to commencing your workout, ensure to check the battery levels in the vest system. Wear the vest and familiarize yourself with the prescribed form. Adjust flex sensor values 1 and 2 as recommended. Set the weight difference on the mats according to our guidelines.

Note: We recommend a maximum weight difference of 10% of the total weight.

Note: Recommended flex sensor values may vary from person to person. Seek guidance from a certified trainer.

Idle Posture

Stand tall with your feet hip-width apart. Keep your shoulders back and relaxed, chin parallel to the ground, and core engaged. Distribute your weight evenly on both feet.

Planks

Start in a push-up position with your hands directly under your shoulders and your body forming a straight line from head to heels. Engage your core muscles to prevent your hips from sagging or lifting too high.

Push-ups

Begin in a plank position with hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart. Lower your body until your chest nearly touches the ground, keeping elbows close to your body. Push back up to starting position while maintaining a straight line from head to heels.

Jumping Jacks

Stand with feet together and arms at your sides. Jump your feet out wide as you raise your arms overhead. Land softly with knees slightly bent and return to starting position.

Bodyweight Squats

Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, toes pointed slightly outward. Lower your body by bending your knees and pushing your hips back as if sitting into a chair. Keep your chest up and back straight, then return to standing position by pushing through your heels.

Walking Lunges

Step forward with one leg, bending both knees to 90 degrees. Keep your torso upright, shoulders back, and core engaged. Alternate legs with each step to maintain balance and stability throughout the movement.
